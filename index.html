The ZX-calculus

The ZX-calculus is a graphical language that goes beyond circuit diagrams. It works by generalising the ideas of the Z and X operations, allowing us to break out of the circuit model while maintaining soundness of reasoning. In doing so we can show properties of circuits, entanglement states, and protocols, in a visually succint but logically complete manner.
